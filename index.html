How to calculate age?

  1. Enter your birth date in the first field
  2. Enter the date you want to calculate your age as of (defaults to today)
  3. Click the Calculate button to see your exact age in years, months, and days

Can I use this to find what day I was born?

Yes. Enter your date of birth and click Calculate. The tool will display the weekday (e.g., Monday).

How can I create a link to share my age?

Enter your birth date and click Share. The generated link will copy to your clipboard.
